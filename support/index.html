Troubleshooting

  • Nothing happens when I click the icon. Some pages cannot be captured for security reasons (browser settings pages, the extension store, local PDF viewers). Try a regular website. If it still fails, reload the tab and try again.
  • The screenshot is not on my clipboard. Clipboard access can be blocked by the page or the operating system. When clipboard writing is unavailable, the image is saved to your downloads folder instead.
  • The stitched image has gaps or repeated sections. Pages with sticky headers, infinite scroll, or heavy animations can confuse stitching. Try again after the page has fully loaded, or collapse sticky elements if possible.
  • The capture stops partway down the page. Very long pages are capped to keep memory use reasonable. Capture the remaining part in a second pass if needed.

Reporting a bug

When reporting an issue, please include your browser and version, the extension version, the URL of the page you tried to capture (if shareable), and what you expected versus what happened. This makes fixes much faster.
