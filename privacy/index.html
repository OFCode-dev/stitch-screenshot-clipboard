Privacy Policy

Stitch Screenshot → Clipboard · Effective date: 2026-07-16

Stitch Screenshot → Clipboard captures a full-page screenshot of the current tab and copies the finished image to your clipboard.

Data collection

We do not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any personal or browsing data.

  • No analytics, telemetry, advertising, or crash reporting.
  • No user accounts, identifiers, or tracking.
  • No screenshot or page content is sent to a server.
  • No remote code is downloaded or executed.

How captures work

Only after you invoke the extension by clicking its toolbar icon or using its keyboard shortcut, the extension temporarily accesses the active tab, scrolls through the page, captures visible sections, and stitches them into one image. All processing happens locally in your browser.

The resulting image is copied to your system clipboard. If clipboard writing is unavailable, the image may be saved to your downloads folder. The extension does not retain the image after the operation.

Permissions

  • activeTab — temporarily accesses only the tab on which you invoked the extension.
  • scripting — injects the temporary scrolling and stitching script into that tab on demand.
  • clipboardWrite — places the finished screenshot on your clipboard.

Third parties

The extension does not use third-party analytics, advertising, tracking services, or external processing services.
