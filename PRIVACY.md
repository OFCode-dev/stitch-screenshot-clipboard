# Privacy Policy — Stitch Screenshot → Clipboard

**Effective date: 2026-07-16**

Stitch Screenshot → Clipboard is a browser extension that captures a full-page screenshot of the current tab and copies it to your clipboard.

## Data collection

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any data. None.**

- No analytics, no telemetry, no crash reporting.
- No user accounts, no identifiers.
- No network requests are made by the extension — it contains no remote code and talks to no server.

## How captures work

When you invoke the extension (toolbar click or keyboard shortcut), the page is captured and stitched **entirely locally in your browser**. The resulting image is placed on your system clipboard (or, if the clipboard is unavailable, saved to your downloads folder). The extension never sees, keeps, or sends the image anywhere; where you paste it is entirely up to you.

## Permissions

- **activeTab** — grants access to the tab you invoked the extension on, only at that moment. The extension has no standing access to any website.
- **scripting** — used to inject the scrolling/stitching script into that tab, on demand only.
- **clipboardWrite** — used to place the finished image on your clipboard.

## Changes

Any change to this policy will be published in this document with an updated effective date. Since the extension collects nothing, meaningful changes are unlikely.
